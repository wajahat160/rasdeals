Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 11, 2025

At RASDEALS, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website www.rasdeals.com, book our services, or interact with us. By using our services, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our tour services, including desert safaris, city tours, and other activities. The types of information we collect include:

  • Personal Information: When you book a tour, contact us, or submit forms, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, country code, and pickup location.
  • Booking Details: Information such as the number of people in your group, selected tour packages, special requests, and payment details (processed securely via third-party payment providers).
  • Usage Data: We collect data about your interactions with our website, such as IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent, using cookies and analytics tools like Google Analytics.
  • Communication Data: Information you provide when contacting us via WhatsApp, email, or phone, including messages and call logs.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and confirm tour bookings.
  • Communicate with you about your bookings, including confirmations, reminders, and updates.
  • Provide customer support via email, WhatsApp, or phone.
  • Improve our website and services through analytics and user feedback.
  • Send promotional offers or newsletters, if you opt-in (you can unsubscribe at any time).
  • Comply with legal obligations or protect our rights.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party providers who assist with booking processing, payment processing, analytics, or communication (e.g., Formspree, WATI, Google Analytics). These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data.
  • Tour Operators: Local partners who operate the tours, receiving only the information necessary to fulfill your booking (e.g., name, group size, pickup location).
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law or to protect our rights, safety, or property.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience and analyze usage. Cookies may include:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for website functionality, such as navigation and booking forms.
  • Analytics Cookies: Track website performance and user behavior (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Marketing Cookies: Used for personalized ads, if you consent.

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures, such as encryption and secure servers, to protect your information. However, no online transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data.
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data, subject to legal obligations.
