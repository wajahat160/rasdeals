About RASDEALS

Welcome to RASDEALS, your premier partner for unforgettable adventures in Dubai and Abu Dhabi. We specialize in crafting premium desert safaris, city tours, water activities, and more, designed to showcase the beauty and culture of the UAE.

Our Mission

At RASDEALS, our mission is to create memorable experiences that blend adventure, comfort, and cultural immersion. We aim to exceed your expectations by offering personalized tours, exceptional service, and a deep respect for the UAE’s heritage and environment.

What We Offer

We provide a wide range of tours and services, including:

  • Desert Safaris: From thrilling evening safaris with dune bashing and BBQ dinners to serene overnight camping under the stars.
  • City Tours: Explore Dubai and Abu Dhabi’s iconic landmarks, from the Burj Khalifa to the Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque.
  • Water Activities: Enjoy yacht cruises, jet skiing, and other exciting water-based adventures.
  • Airport Transfers: Seamless and comfortable transfers to and from Dubai and Abu Dhabi airports.
  • Dune Buggy Rides: Experience the thrill of off-road adventures in the desert.

Why Choose RASDEALS?

We stand out for our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. Here’s why travelers trust us:

  • Expert Guides: Our knowledgeable and friendly guides ensure a safe and enriching experience.
  • Premium Service: From comfortable vehicles to curated itineraries, we prioritize your comfort and enjoyment.
  • Safety First: We adhere to strict safety standards, partnering with licensed operators to ensure your well-being.
  • Customer Support: Our team is available via WhatsApp, email, or phone to assist you at every step.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

We are dedicated to preserving the UAE’s natural beauty and cultural heritage. We work with local communities, promote eco-friendly practices, and encourage responsible tourism to ensure our tours have a positive impact.
