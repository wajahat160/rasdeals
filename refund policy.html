Refund Policy

At RASDEALS, we strive to ensure your satisfaction. Please review our refund policy below for details on cancellations and refunds.

Cancellation Policy

Cancellations must be made at least 48 hours prior to the scheduled tour date to be eligible for a refund. Cancellations within 48 hours will not be eligible for a refund unless due to exceptional circumstances (e.g., severe weather conditions).

Refund Process

Refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days after receiving your cancellation request. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used during booking.

A 10% processing fee may be deducted from the refund amount to cover administrative costs.

Non-Refundable Cases

No refunds will be provided for:

  • Cancellations made less than 48 hours before the tour.
  • No-shows or failure to attend the scheduled tour.
  • Bookings made with special discounts or promotional offers unless specified otherwise.

Exceptional Circumstances

In cases of force majeure (e.g., natural disasters, government restrictions), RASDEALS will assess the situation and may offer a full or partial refund or reschedule the tour at no additional cost.
